01 · The Opportunity
One place to
run your studio.

Kattitude Tattoo Studio has built its reputation on the craft. But behind the scenes, client sessions are tracked on paper sheets, tips are totaled by hand, payments run through multiple processors with no single view, and a monthly Shopify subscription charges you to sell two products online.

This proposal lays out a custom command center dashboard and a clean exit from Shopify — one authenticated system where your daily operations, your revenue, and your shop all live together. Built on the same Vercel + Supabase stack we run for THE LOST+UNFOUNDS.

02 · What You Get
  • ■ Client Session Log — Digitizes the daily paper sheet. Each session captures client name, service, tattoo price, tip amount, and payment method. Stored permanently and searchable — you'll have historical records you've never had before.
  • ■ Revenue Dashboard — Daily, weekly, and monthly totals with breakdowns by payment processor and artist. Total take-in, total tips, average session value — at a glance, at the end of every day.
  • ■ Custom Shop (Shopify Exit) — Your products rebuilt into your own stack. Same checkout experience, zero monthly platform fee. You stop paying Shopify the day the dashboard goes live.
  • ■ Quick-Launch Panel — One-click access to your booking platform, email, social accounts, and supplier tools. Everything inside one authenticated, mobile-ready dashboard.
  • ■ Branded & Secured — The entire dashboard is skinned to Kattitude's colors, fonts, and logo. Protected by secure login. You own the source code and all your data outright.
Also Included
  • ■ Deployed to Vercel — live URL from day one, your own domain when you're ready to point it.
  • ■ Mobile-responsive — fully usable from your phone at the end of a session.
  • ■ 30 days of post-launch support included in the build fee.
  • ■ Full data export available any time — your records, your format, no lock-in.
