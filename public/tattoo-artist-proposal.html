01 · The Opportunity

You create the art. Your website brings them in.

Independent tattoo artists build their reputation one piece at a time — but the booking process often lives scattered across Instagram DMs, phone tag, and word of mouth. A dedicated website changes that. It puts your portfolio, your booking flow, and your deposit collection in one place that works for you around the clock, not just when you're online to answer a message.

This is a website built specifically for how independent artists work: a clean portfolio that shows off your best pieces, a booking system that turns interest into scheduled sessions, and a dashboard where you manage it all from your phone or a laptop.

I built this platform for my own business and it's been running in production. You get a custom version of that same system, shaped around your style and your studio.

What changes for you
  • Bookings come through the system — Clients submit requests with references and style details directly through your site. No more losing track of a DM from three weeks ago.
  • Deposits are handled automatically — Payment collection is built into the booking flow. Your spot is secured before it hits your calendar.
  • Your portfolio speaks for itself online — A gallery built to show your work at its best, available any time someone wants to see what you do — not just when they catch your latest post.

03 · How It Works Day to Day

From first click to final session.

The platform is built to guide a client from discovering your work to sitting in your chair — with every step tracked along the way.

The Client Journey
  • A client finds your website — through search, a shared link, or your social profile.
  • They browse your gallery and get a feel for your style and past work.
  • They submit a booking request with reference photos and style details.
  • They pay a deposit to secure the appointment.
Your Side
  • You review the request in your dashboard, with the deposit already confirmed.
  • You approve, schedule, and prepare for the session with everything you need already on file.
  • The session happens.
  • The client's record stays in your system, ready for the next time they book.
Add-On Features
Once your core site is live, additional features are available whenever you want them — flash sale pages, a merch storefront, email marketing, AI-powered booking estimates, digital waivers, or multi-artist support for a shared studio. Each add-on is scoped and quoted before any work begins, starting at $300 per feature.
