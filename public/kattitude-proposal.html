03 · Technical Scope

What the AI booking system actually does.

This is a custom estimation engine built on AI image analysis, historical session data, and per-artist performance tracking. Here is the full product requirements breakdown.

AI Image Analysis
  • Reference photo processing — client uploads 1–5 reference images. The AI analyzes complexity (linework density, shading, color count), estimates surface area based on placement selection, and classifies the style (traditional, realism, blackwork, watercolor, etc.).
  • Complexity scoring — each piece gets a 1–10 complexity score that maps to time estimates. Fine-line portrait realism scores higher than a simple flash piece. The score is transparent to the client.
Artist Performance Tracking
  • Speed profiles — each artist's average time per complexity point is tracked across completed sessions. Artist A might average 45 min per point, Artist B might average 30 min. Quotes adjust per artist.
  • Session logging — check-in time, check-out time, break time, cleanup time. Builds a real dataset that improves estimates over time.
  • Availability engine — each artist sets their working hours. The system accounts for existing bookings, estimated session length, and a configurable buffer (cleanup/reset time between clients — default 30 minutes).
Booking Flow
  • Step 1: Client selects tattoo style and body placement
  • Step 2: Client uploads reference photos
  • Step 3: AI generates estimate (time range + price range)
  • Step 4: Client selects preferred artist or "first available"
  • Step 5: Calendar shows real availability based on estimate duration
  • Step 6: Client books and pays deposit to confirm
In-Studio Operations
  • Point-of-sale — process final payments, tips, and merch purchases on any device. Works on a phone, tablet, or shop computer.
  • Artist check-in kiosk — simple clock-in/clock-out screen. Tap your name, start your shift. Log breaks. Clock out when done. Runs in the browser on any device — zero setup for artists.
