Your art on display. Your brand in their hands.

An interactive kiosk turns a waiting room, lobby, or retail floor into an always-on showcase. Clients browse a full portfolio, explore flash sheets, scan a QR code to book, and check pricing — hands-free for your staff, running the whole time the doors are open.

It gives every visitor a professional, branded experience the moment they walk in — a polished display that stands in for a printed binder, a phone handed across the counter, or a question sent to the front desk. The kiosk is always there, always current, and always on-brand.

Once it is installed, it runs itself. Content updates happen remotely, and the display keeps working around the clock without anyone tending to it.

03 · How It Works

Plug in. Power on. It runs itself.

From power-on to closing time, the kiosk manages itself — here is what a day with it actually looks like.

  • The kiosk powers on with the PC and boots directly into fullscreen mode — no login, no setup, no staff involvement.
  • A branded splash screen greets every visitor as the display comes to life.
  • Clients tap to browse, swiping through the gallery at their own pace.
  • A tap zooms into any piece for a closer look at detail and line work.
  • A QR code on screen links straight to your booking page.
  • After two minutes idle, the screensaver takes over and auto-cycles through your content.
  • A tap anywhere wakes the display and picks right back up.
Built for the real world
The system is designed to keep running without anyone watching it: it auto-restarts after a power outage, uses Wake Lock to keep the display from sleeping, has pinch-zoom disabled to prevent accidental gestures, and activates the screensaver automatically during idle periods. It is built for environments where nobody is standing by to babysit the screen.
