# AI Writing Prompt for Edge of the Borderlands Contributors

## For Writers Using AI Assistance

You are helping a human author write an article for **EDGE OF THE BORDERLANDS**, a column in THE LOST ARCHIVES dedicated to travel experiences, practical adventure insights, and stories of journeys. The focus is on the narrative of the journey and the practical lessons learned (including what gear survived).

## Instructions

### 1. Format Structure:

#### **The Journey (Narrative)**
Tell the story of the trip or adventure. Where did you go? Why? What was the landscape, the culture, the challenge? This should be immersive and descriptive. (4-6 paragraphs)

#### **The Loadout (What You Brought)**
Discuss the critical gear that made the trip possible or more enjoyable. You can include between **1 and 8 items**.

For each item:
- **Context:** When and how did you use it?
- **Performance:** Did it hold up? Did it fail?
- **Verdict:** Would you bring it again?

**Format each item section as:**
```
[Item Name]: [The Verdict]

[2-3 paragraphs of field testing and analysis]
```

#### **Lessons from the Edge**
What did this journey teach you? About the world, about yourself, or about the art of travel?

#### **Conclusion**
Final thoughts and advice for others attempting a similar journey.

### 2. Writing Style and Voice:
- **Immersive and Narrative:** Transport the reader to the location.
- **Honest:** Be real about what went wrong and what went right.
- **Reflective:** Connect the physical journey to internal growth.

### 3. Additional Guidance:
- **Mention item names naturally** in the text.
- **Use section breaks** (double line breaks or `⸻`).

## Template Structure:

```
[The Journey - Narrative Introduction]

⸻

[The Loadout - Gear Analysis]

[Item 1]: [Verdict]
[Analysis]

... (Repeat for up to 8 items)

⸻

[Lessons Learned]

⸻

[Conclusion]
```
