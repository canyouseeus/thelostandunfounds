**Subject:** A whole lot is new at THE LOST+UNFOUNDS — come see

---

What up, fam.

We've been heads-down for a minute, and the site is in a different place
than the last time a lot of you visited. This isn't a "we moved a button"
update — there's actual new stuff to do here now. Here's where things
stand today and what's worth checking out.

**Shop your event photos — and pay how you want**

The galleries got a real storefront. If you were shot at an event, you can
browse your gallery, pick the photos you want, and check out on the spot.
Every gallery is priced on its own, and you can pay with a card or with
Bitcoin over the Lightning Network. Once you've paid, your photos drop into
a download portal — full-resolution, yours to keep.

**The Shop + the Mystery Box**

The Shop is live and easy to check out from — card payments, no hoops. The
one to look at first is the Mystery Box: you don't know exactly what's
coming, but you know it's us. It's the kind of thing that's more fun than
it has any right to be.

**The Affiliate Program & King Midas**

You can now sign up as an affiliate, share your link, and earn commission
on what comes through it. Payouts run through Stripe, so getting paid is
straightforward — connect your account and you're set. On top of that
there's King Midas, our leaderboard and gamification layer, so repping the
brand actually means something on the board. If you've ever wanted to be
part of this beyond being a customer, this is the door.

**The Tool Box**

We've been quietly stacking up free tools you can just use — like the
TikTok Downloader — all in one Tool Box on the site. No login wall, no
catch. Go poke around.

**An interactive map**

Photos and events now plot onto a map at the actual venue they happened
at, so you can explore the city through what we've shot rather than just
scrolling a feed.

---

That's the current state of the site. None of this is "coming soon" — it's
all live right now. Head over, click into a gallery, grab a Mystery Box, or
just see what the Tool Box can do for you.

Visit: https://www.thelostandunfounds.com

The only way to go is up.

— THE LOST+UNFOUNDS
